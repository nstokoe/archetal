PRIVATE SCHOOL:
WASHINGTON, DC

Client: The Lowell School
Size: 12000 sq.ft.
Completed:1989

Used as a primary school for 120 students, this project involved the renovation and addition to the historic Himmelfarb Mansion in Washington, DC. It was completely modernized, while
maintaining the historic character of the original house. The project received a Washington Chapter AIA Award of Excellence.
