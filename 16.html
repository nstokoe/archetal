BEACH HOUSE:
NORTHUMBERLAND COUNTY
VIRGINIA

Client: Withheld
Size: 4000 sq.ft.
Completed: 1991

Located on the Virginia side of the Potomac River this retirement home was designed to maximize views and breezes off the water. The massing of the house was shaped to make the house a landmark both from the water and the land while tempering its size with a one room deep partial second floor. Brick walls on the east and west bays reflect on the 18th century traditions of Virginia.
