MUSEUM SHOP:
NATIONAL AIR AND SPACE MUSEUM

Client: Smithsonian Inst.
Size: 12,800 sq.ft.
Completed: 2000

The project scope included the remodeling and expansion of the existing shop into the basement parking level of the museum. The goals of the project were to provide increases in revenue along with significant improvements in the accommodation of patrons and internal operations. The new design maintains an appropriate character for its museum setting, while upgrading its appeal to the visitor with new graphics, the integration of objects from the collections, and state of the art audio visual systems.
