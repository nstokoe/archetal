MURALS:
NATIONAL ARCHIVES

Client: National Archives and Records Administration
Completed: 2006

This project included the design of six large themed murals and their installation on the vestibule walls of the theater level of the National Archives building in Washington, DC. The photographs and images of documents are displayed to give the visitor a sampling of the holdings of the National Archives. Mural titles are displayed as cut-out letters in gold anodized aluminum panels.
