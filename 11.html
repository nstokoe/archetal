EXHIBIT: ENTERPRISE STUDIO MODEL, NATIONAL AIR AND SPACE MUSEUM

Client: Smithsonian
Size: 225 sq.ft.
Completed: 2000

This project includes the design of a specialized free-standing display case for a large scale model of the Starship Enterprise. The model is suspended in a glass “room” that allows viewing from all sides within an expanded museum store space. Fiber optic lighting is incorporated to light the artifact from above and below. A graphic mural on an adjacent wall provides a visual backdrop.
