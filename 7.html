APARTMENT DESIGN

Client: Withheld
Size: 5,000 sq.ft.
Completed: 2000

The design for this twelve room apartment was crafted to create a museum quality setting for art and a pleasing and efficient setting for living and entertaining. The architectural character of the final design was the result of a close collaboration with the client and combines aspects of oriental art and the work of the architects Greene and Greene. Deep walls and hidden doors in the stained walnut millwork provide spaces for storage and sophisticated lighting and audio visual control systems.
