STUDIO:
WASHINGTON, DC

Client: Withheld
Size: 450 sq.ft.
Completed: 2006

The studio was designed to replace an existing garage with writing and family space as well as a much needed storage/workshop room. Located to the rear of an historic bungalow within sight of the National Cathedral from the large window, the project becomes a backyard getaway and a backdrop for outdoor living and play space.
