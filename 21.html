MUSEUM SHOP:
HISTORIC MOUNT VERNON

Client: Historic Mount Vernon
Size: 6500 sq.ft.
Completed: 2001

The project involves more than doubling and a complete renovation of an existing shop. The design creates two retail spaces. One features primarily children’s merchandise and a Christmas Shop and the other includes a large book department, jewelry, food products, gifts and reproductions. Specially fabricated store fixtures are finished with opaque stains that reflect original colors from George Washington's time. Graphic display panels were created to make connections between products and the history of the estate.
