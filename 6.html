EXHIBIT GRAPHICS:
RENOIR TO ROTHKO

Client: Phillips Collection
Exhibit Area: 17000 sq.ft.
Completed: 1999

The graphics and signage
of the exhibition logo were used on the exterior of the museum to not only announce the exhibit but also to redirect the traffic flow to the original house entry—the special entrance for this exhibition.

Extensive graphics included the design of the exhibition logo, banners, press kit package and text panels throughout the exhibit to help tell the rich story of Duncan Phillips and his museum. This was the first exhibit at The Phillips to use both buildings.
