BOOKSTORE:
NATIONAL MUSEUM OF
AMERICAN ART

Client: Smithsonian
Size: 1800 sq.ft.
Completed: 1991

The bookstore included
the expansion and complete redesign of a small museum bookstore in the National Museum of American Art, Smithsonian Institution.
The principal feature of the design is the creation of a curving wall of niches and a frieze decorated with artists’ quotes on the subject of art.

The casework, including the facing of the niche wall and frieze, was designed as “furniture” to create a clear distinction between store elements and the elegantly simple character of this historic building.
